## Support model template

| Tier | Example | Response objective | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| P1 | breach/full outage/financial integrity | immediate in agreed coverage | supplier + client IT |
| P2 | critical workflow blocked | agreed business-hour target | supplier |
| P3 | workaround exists | planned support | supplier/client |
| P4 | minor enhancement | backlog | governance |

## Client dependencies

Secure connectivity, named business owners/approvers, legal authority for data access, hardware/printer/QR readiness, WINSpeed DBA/accounting participation, timely UAT/decision feedback.

## Warranty boundary

Warranty covers agreed deliverables meeting signed acceptance criteria in supported environment. Excludes third-party outages/change, legacy data correction outside scope, unauthorized direct DB changes, new requirements after acceptance, and unmanaged hardware/network faults.

## Handover

Contracted artefacts, release record, runbook, access-transfer checklist, open-issue register and acceptance sign-off.
