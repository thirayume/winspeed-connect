## Training principle

Train by role and scenario, including rejected verification, unlock, ambiguous TruckScale, missing paper and rebate issue.

| Role | Duration | Outcome |
|---|---:|---|
| SALES | 2–3h | SO, rebate preview/claim, giveaway, unlock |
| COUNTER_SALES | 3h | verify, confirm, print/paper, exceptions |
| WAREHOUSE | 2h | picking/load/ticket/paper |
| WEIGHBRIDGE | 2–3h | lookup/fallback/ship/reconcile |
| ACCOUNTING | 3h | claim/CN/reconciliation/report |
| APPROVER/MANAGER | 2h | plan/allocation/approval/dashboard |
| ADMIN/IT | 4h | user/master/health/release/incident |

## Competency

Complete scenario unassisted, pass role checklist, know escalation, sign attendance; retrain after material workflow change.

## Hypercare

Named support/floor-walker, daily issue triage, daily reconciliation/status, end-of-day feedback and weekly governance until stabilised.
